Refund Policy

Overview

At Online Clothing Store, we are committed to providing our customers with high-quality products. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, we offer a comprehensive refund policy to ensure your satisfaction.

Eligibility for Refunds

  • The item must be returned within 30 days of the purchase date.
  • The item must be in its original condition, with tags attached and in the original packaging.
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" are not eligible for refunds.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, follow these steps:

  • Contact our customer service team at clothing@verstyle.com with your order number and reason for the refund.
  • Ship the item back to us using a tracked shipping service. Please keep your receipt as proof of shipping.
  • Once we receive the returned item, we will process your refund to the original payment method. This may take 5-10 business days to reflect in your account.

Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges. If you wish to exchange an item, please follow the refund process and place a new order for the item you wish to receive.
