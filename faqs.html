Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • 1. What is your return policy?

    We offer a 30-day return policy on most items. Items must be returned in their original condition, including tags and packaging. Please visit our Returns & Exchanges page for more details.

  • 2. How do I track my order?

    You can track your order using the tracking number provided in your shipping confirmation email. Simply enter the tracking number on our Track Your Order page for the latest updates.

  • 3. What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept various payment methods including credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and other major payment providers. For more information, please check our Payment Options page.

  • 4. How do I change or cancel my order?

    To change or cancel your order, please contact our Customer Service team as soon as possible. Once an order is processed, changes or cancellations may not be possible.

  • 5. Do you offer international shipping?

    Yes, we offer international shipping to most countries. Shipping fees and delivery times vary based on your location. Please refer to our Shipping Information page for more details.

  • 6. How can I contact customer service?

    You can contact our Customer Service team via email at support@example.com or by calling our hotline at +1234567890. For more options, please visit our Contact Us page.

  • 7. What should I do if I receive a damaged or incorrect item?

    If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact our Customer Service team immediately. We will arrange for a replacement or refund as per our Returns & Exchanges policy.

  • 8. How do I use a discount code?

    Enter your discount code at checkout in the "Promo Code" field. The discount will be applied to your order total. Ensure that your code is valid and applied before completing your purchase.
